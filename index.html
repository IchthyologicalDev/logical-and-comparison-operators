Logical and Comparison Operators Exercise

Description

Welcome to the logical and comparison operators exercise! You'll get to practice writing functions that use some common logical and comparison operators. Good luck!

Instructions

  1. Open up index.js in your editor.
  2. Follow the instructions in the comment at the top of that file.
  3. Refresh this page after saving changes to see your progress.
