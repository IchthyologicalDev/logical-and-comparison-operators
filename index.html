Arithmetic Operators Exercise

Description

Welcome to the arithmetic operators exercise! You'll get to practice writing functions that use some common arithmetic operators. Good luck!

Instructions

  1. Open up index.js in your editor.
  2. Follow the instructions in the comment at the top of that file.
  3. Refresh this page after saving changes to see your progress.

Questions

My calculator is broken. When I try to do 5 + 5, it returns 55.

In my fishing guide service, I have a unique way of calculating tips. I round every guide's tips down to the nearest 10, and give the excess to the boat cleaner.

I took out a couple interest only loans to launch this guide service. I think that I'm being overcharged. Help me out! If I just pay the interest amount, what should my monthly payments be?

Marginal tax rates can be confusing. What percent am I actually paying?

I like helping out other small business owners. I am loaning between $500 and $2000 to help someone launch their pizza company. We haven't worked out the interest rate yet, but they guaranteed that they will pay it back in full 1 year later. I need an IOU message from this person.

I have 1 bait bucket per client. I want an equal amount of bait in each bucket at the start of the day. How many pieces of bait will be left over after placing an equal amount in each bucket?

I get calls when the fishing heats up in certain areas. Can I make it to the new location within 30 minutes?

Many fish populations are dwindling. Assuming that each fish can produce 5 fish per year, how many fish would we have in the future if fish were immortal?
